Tajweed

Advanced Course in Classical Arabic Reading

"Tajweed: Advanced Course in Classical Arabic Reading" is an ongoing course led by the brother Noor in Polimi. This initiative promoted by Asm is allowing many students to learn and improve their Arabic reading. We are very glad to see the participants so eager and impatient to learn more and more. This is the inauguration of a future chain of projects targeting the cultural enrichment of students, insha' Allah, for learning is a never ending process;
                                                           لا يَنْبَغِي لأَحَدٍ يَكُونُ عِنْدَهُ الْعِلْمُ أَنْ يَتْرُكَ التَّعَلُّمَ
It is not befitting for anyone with knowledge to give up learning.
Source: Jāmi’ Bayān al-‘Ilm 423
The course is held weekly, every Thursday and Friday from 6:30 pm till 8 pm and it's open to all students. If you want to join us please feel free to contact us.
