18th April 2018

Conference on "Halal Food"

On 18th April, ASM arranged a conference on Halal Food held by the Sheikh Mamadou Sylla, managing director of the religious committee of World Halal Authority, president of the Senegalese community in Italy and imam in different Islamic centres. During this beneficial meeting the Sheikh started by focusing on the general meaning of the term "Halal" on a religious level and then went deep inside the wide topic of food touching some important aspects, answering questions and solving the doubts arisen among the audience. 

24th March 2018

meet ASM during the open day in Polimi 

During the open day in Polimi ( 24th March), ASM got the opportunity to introduce itself to the new future students of the University, presenting its program, aspirations and principles. It was a good opportunity to prove ASM's active presence in the academic environment.

2th February 2018

Conference about "Leadership and Company Management "

In collaboration with Indonesian Students Association in Italy (PPI), ASM organised a conference about Leadership and Company Management held by the author of the books "Sharing" and "Sharing 2", Dr. Handry Satriago, CEO of General Electric Indonesia ( American multinational corporation founded in New York, 1892). Dr Satriago is currently the Chief Executive Officer for GE Indonesia, the first "Made in Indonesia" GE leader. Since he was recorded as the youngest CEO in GE Global, he's the right inspiration to the audiences who wish to develop themselves to become strong leaders.
