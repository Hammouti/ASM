Umrah

Asm succeeded in arranging a Umrah (26th February - 7th March) as the very first humble event of the Association. The trip, which was addressed to students only, was the best way for ASM to start its journey and exhibit the noble causes and values it wishes to embody and, as regards the participants, it represented a great opportunity to strengthen their Imaan and, for some, visit the Holy cities of Mecca and Madinah for the very first time.

ASM managed to offer a highly discounted price and for the students who got a first-class honours Degree the entire Umrah package was free. In addition to seven polimi students, approximately thirty more students from all over Italy performed Umrah, returning soul-enriched, satisfied and grateful. Alhamdulillah.
