Canoa & Dragon Boat
Learning to Canoe and developing your team work skills will be the aims of the activity day. The activity consist of two parts: the Canoe and Dragon Boat. The experience in Canoe includes 30 minutes of theoretical lesson and a subsequent practical lesson in the water with the presence of an instructor for every 10 participants. Dragon Boat, a discipline of Chinese origin, is a group experience in which teams of 10 students will compete in a series of competitions. There will be a 30-minute theory lesson followed by the water practice lesson. The instructor will give instructions to carry out the activity in maximum safety.

REGISTRATIONS OPEN FROM MONDAY 17/09 to 8:00 PM

Day: Saturday 29 September 2018 ;
Where: Idroscalo Club, you can reach there using bus n°73;
Cost: Free, you must pay € 20 deposit which will be returned                 later ;
Number of students: 30 of Politecnico di Milano;
Program of the day:
- 10: 30-12: 45 canoe ;
- lunch break (food not included in the price) ;
- 15: 00-16: 45 dragon boat.

Advice for the participants :
It is recommended to wear sweatshirt, water-proof jacket, waterproof long trousers, slippers. It is essential to bring the appropriate and complete set spare clothes and towel.
